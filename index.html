Our Mission

The traditional lumber industry is broken. Wood is grown and harvested and milled locally, transported on a ship, which travels thousands of miles to be processed and treated, it then travels back thousands of miles and sits in a massive warehouse until someone is ready to buy it.


We’re on a mission to fix this broken industry and we have some of the most innovative companies on the planet helping us.
